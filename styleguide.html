Layout

The three containers

Every case study is built from these. The page is a grid with two invisible lanes: everything lands in the text lane by default, and anything tagged cs-wide stretches to the wide lane. Grouped text sits in cards.

wide lane · 976px content · add cs-wide (the ToE comparison slider lives here)
text lane · 712px content · the default for everything

card

The text container: soft surface, radius-lg corners, space-24 padding. Change cards, stat blocks, and experience entries are all this card plus their own layout inside.

Beyond the shared system

Page-specific patterns

These live inside their own page, not in the shared files. Name them and I'll know where to look: the stat grids (cs-stats on Bonuses, cs-stat-pills on ToE), the phone screen rows (cs-stage, cs-pair), the before/after/why cards (cs-baw-grid on ToE), the experiment order diagram (cs-order-grid on ToE), the deep-dive pockets (cs-deep on ToE), the drag comparison slider (cs-comparison on ToE), the experience entries (exp-entry), and the homepage snapshots (snapshot).
